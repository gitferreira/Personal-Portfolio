About Me

Coding my way to the Top

I always thought my dream was to get into the tech industry; working at my 3 monitor setup, playing some ping-pong after the coffee-break and starting conversations with potential investors for the new Facebook, AirBnb or whatever groundbreaking product the company was developing. It seemed to be an appropiate dream given my bachellor in Business Management, from which I gravitated towards Innovation management. I got my Masters degree on the topic and started working for innovative and tech-savvy companies. But I kind of just felt that i needed more, that I yearned to learn more, and to learn something different.

And then it happened,the spark I was missing lighted up the second I clicked play on my first Javascript tutorial video. Since then, I just can´t enough. FullStack development calls to all my passions; incorporating creativity and analytical thinking to problem solving to create distinctly unique solutions.

I decided to pursue a Web-app development bootcamp called IronHack which I had been highly recommended, and in the span of three months I had HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, React, Redux, Jasmine, Bootstrap, Node.JS, MongoDB, Express.JS, Git/GitHub, Heroku and Digital Ocean under my belt. I´ve started learning AngularJS and giving my first steps with Python and I´m eager to grab other programming languages, frameworks or principles I can integrate into my projects.

I love the principles of responsiveness and the oddly satisfying feeling when watching websites shrink into mobile screens to still look amazing. I like to see myself like those web pages, moldable but still keeping my creative twist.
